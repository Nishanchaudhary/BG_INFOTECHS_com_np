FAQ'S

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our email marketing services.

How quickly can we see results from email marketing?
Most clients see measurable improvements within the first 30 days, with open rates typically increasing by 20-50% in the first campaign. Full optimization takes 3-6 months as we refine segmentation and messaging.
Do you provide email templates or custom designs?
We offer both! Choose from our library of professionally designed templates or work with our designers to create completely custom email designs that match your brand perfectly.
How do you ensure our emails don't go to spam?
We implement multiple strategies including proper authentication (SPF, DKIM, DMARC), maintaining clean lists, gradual list warming, content optimization, and monitoring blacklists. Our deliverability rate averages 98%.
Can you integrate with our CRM or ecommerce platform?
Yes, we integrate with all major platforms including Salesforce, HubSpot, Shopify, WooCommerce, Magento, and more. We can also build custom integrations for proprietary systems.
